Responsible Buy Now, Pay Later

Embedding Long-Term Financial Responsibility into Short-Term Consumption

Author: Adam Schofield
Status: Concept White Paper
Date: 19 January 2026

This site hosts the original white paper outlining Responsible BNPL — a behaviourally-aligned redesign of Buy Now, Pay Later systems in which the first payment is diverted into a ring-fenced Future Self Fund, accumulating across purchases for long-term outcomes such as housing deposits or retirement savings.

The paper is published publicly to establish authorship, invite critique, and contribute constructively to discussions around financial literacy, consumer protection, and responsible credit design.
